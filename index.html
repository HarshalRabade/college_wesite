Post Graduation

At Eduford graduate degree requirements are determined by the individual departments or programs and approved by the Committee on Graduate Programs (CGP). Each graduate student is officially enrolled in an individual degree program. Eduford graduate programs are full-time and work is done chiefly on campus in collaboration with faculty, peers, and the Institute community.
